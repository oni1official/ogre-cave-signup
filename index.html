Send It to the Cave

The Ogre Cave is Oni-1's live, radio-style broadcast from Cyber City: a place for conversation, community chaos, creativity, gaming, music, internet culture, and the underground topics that deserve more light.

As the Voice of the Underground, Oni-1 wants the Cave to be a place where the audience can help point the spotlight. Your idea does not have to be huge, polished, or perfect. If you think it could start a good conversation, make people laugh, help someone discover something new, or give an overlooked topic a little more light, send it in.

Submit a topic, project, creator, discovery, question, or guest idea. As long as it fits the guidelines, all kinds of discussions are welcome here: fun, weird, creative, thoughtful, niche, personal, underground, or just something you think deserves to be heard.
Good Submissions Include
  • Topics: Questions, opinions, debates, funny observations, internet weirdness, or culture discussions worth bringing up live.
  • Discoveries: Indie games, music, creators, tools, videos, communities, stories, or projects that deserve attention.
  • Your Own Work: Music, streams, art, games, writing, events, or anything you're building and want to share.
  • Guest Ideas: Let Oni-1 know if you or someone else could be a good fit for a future featured conversation.
Keep the Signal Clean
  • No hate or harassment. Bigotry, targeted attacks, or cruel behavior are not welcome.
  • No doom spirals. Heavy topics can exist, but the Cave is not built for trauma dumping or extreme negativity.
  • Bring context if you can. A few details help Oni-1 understand why the topic matters, but you do not need to write a full essay.
  • Submission does not guarantee coverage. Oni-1 may adjust, combine, save, or skip topics depending on fit and timing.

No pressure to make it perfect. Drop your signal below. The Cave is listening.

Tip: Small ideas are welcome too. A quick spark can still turn into a great Ogre Cave conversation.
